Major project

Simulation study of low-velocity impact on FRPs — Bachelor Thesis

BMS College of Engineering · 2020

Built explicit finite-element models of AS4/8552 laminates in Abaqus/Explicit. Modeled matrix cracking and fibre failure (Hashin / Puck) and inter-ply delamination with cohesive interfaces and tie constraints. Correlated force–time, displacement–time, and damage patterns with published benchmarks across impact energies.
